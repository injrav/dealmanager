### Sample Config:
```xml
<web-ext xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance"
         xmlns="http://websphere.ibm.com/xml/ns/javaee"
         xsi:schemaLocation="http://websphere.ibm.com/xml/ns/javaee 
         ibm-web-ext_1_0.xsd"
         version="1.0">

  <session-management>
    <cookie-config>
      <domain>sub.example.com</domain>
    </cookie-config>
  </session-management>

</web-ext>
```

- This overrides the default domain for session cookies (like `JSESSIONID`).
- **Avoid using `.example.com`** unless multi-subdomain sharing is intentional.
